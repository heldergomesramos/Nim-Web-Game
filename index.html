Nim is a simple tactical game for two players. Each player removes one or more balls from each column and the objective is to be the one that removes the last ball on the board.

When you remove a ball in the middle of a column, every ball above it is also removed. To remove balls, simply click on them. You will see exactly which balls will be removed as they will shake.

Difficulty

  1. AI just plays random moves
  2. Easy mode, if you are having some trouble
  3. Normal mode, recommended for beginners
  4. So, you want to do this the hard way...
  5. You cannot win

Board Size: Choose the number of columns in the game. The balls are automatically generated
